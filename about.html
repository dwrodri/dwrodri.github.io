About Me

I'm a current member of the NUCAR Lab at Northeastern University. Before that, I got my B.Sc. in Computer Science at Clemson University.

When I'm not programming, working on school, or writing stuff to post here, I like to record and produce music. I started off with electronic music as a teenager, but have since expanded into jazz and rock/metal. When I'm not making music, I'm gaming, playing squash, skateboarding, or looking for a warm tropical beach to sit on.
